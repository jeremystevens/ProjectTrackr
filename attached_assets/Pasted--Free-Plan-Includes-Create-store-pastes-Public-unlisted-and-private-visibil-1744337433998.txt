🆓 Free Plan
-------------
Includes:
- Create & store pastes
- Public, unlisted, and private visibility
- Self-destruct option
- Basic syntax highlighting
- Access and manage your own pastes

⚙️ Starter AI – $5/month
------------------------
Includes all Free features, plus:
- AI-Generated Tags
- AI Fix Suggestions (lite)
- AI Search (limited queries)
- Per-Paste Analytics (views + basic source info)
- Follower Count / Trending Pastes (read-only)

🚀 Pro AI – $10/month
---------------------
Includes everything in Starter, plus:
- Full AI Code Refactoring
- Advanced AI Search (more queries + smarter results)
- Scheduled Paste Publishing
- Custom Syntax Themes (Monokai, Dracula, etc.)
- Paste Access Insight (location, device info – no IP address)
- Private Comments on pastes

👥 Dev Team – $25/month
------------------------
Includes everything in Pro AI, plus:
- Live Collaborative Pastes (real-time editing)
- Team seat sharing (up to 5 users)
- Full analytics suite (geolocation + referral breakdown)
- Edit/view/manage all team pastes
- API/Webhook Access (future upgrade)
